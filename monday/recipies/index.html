Instrucciones de preparacion

  1. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium high heat until hot.
  2. Add fillets of cod and squeeze 1/2 of the lemon’s juice over the top of each.
  3. Sprinkle with pepper to taste.
  4. Cook for 4 minutes and turn.
  5. Squeeze with the remaining lemon’s juice and sprinkle with pepper to taste.
  6. Continue to cook until fillets flake easily with a fork.
  7. Serve with rice and a side of salad.
  8. final

Ingredientes

  • 2 Fillets of Cod
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1/4 tbsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
